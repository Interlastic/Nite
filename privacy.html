Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Nite is a Discord bot operated by an individual (the "operator"). This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled when you use Nite.

2. Data We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Discord Data: When you interact with Nite, we may access your Discord username and user ID.
  • Messages: Messages you send directly to Nite for AI chat features may be processed to generate responses.
  • Server Settings: Server administrators may configure server-specific settings such as welcome messages.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Basic Usage Data: We track the number of interactions per user for rate limiting purposes.
  • Command Usage: Anonymous aggregate counts of command usage may be collected for feature improvement.

AI Processing

  • External AI Services: When using Nite's AI chat features, your prompts are sent to third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI's or Gemini's API) for processing. We do not control and are not responsible for the data practices of these providers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use data to:

  • Provide and maintain Nite's features
  • Process AI chat requests
  • Prevent abuse through rate limiting
  • Improve our services

4. Data Storage

  • User Information: We do NOT maintain persistent user profiles. Basic interaction counts are stored per-user for rate limiting only.
  • Server Settings: Server administrators can request deletion of server-specific settings by contacting the operator.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or otherwise share user data with third parties, except:

  • AI Service Providers: When you use AI features, your prompts are sent to external AI APIs
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted here.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the operator through Discord: @interlastical
